## 📝 App Summary (Store Description)

### What is Sankalp?
Sankalp is a cinematic discipline companion designed for high-performers, students, and anyone seeking to reclaim their focus. Unlike traditional habit trackers that feel like spreadsheets, Sankalp provides an immersive, physics-based environment that treats your discipline as a living energy.

### Who is it for?
- Individuals overcoming distractions and digital noise.
- High-performers looking to gamify their consistency.
- Users who value extreme privacy (No cloud, no tracking).

### Key Benefits:
- **Physics-Driven UI:** Experience your progress through a premium, tactile interface that responds to your touch.
- **Dynamic Discipline Score:** Move beyond simple numbers. Our engine analyzes your "victory rate" to provide a holistic view of your resolve.
- **The Clarity Hub:** Don't just track—learn. Get targeted recommendations from classical and modern literature based on your current state of mind.
- **Control Mode:** A dedicated emergency interface for high-risk moments, providing immediate protocols to maintain your streak.
- **Multilingual Support:** Fully localized in English and Hindi to support your journey in the language that resonates most.

### Privacy First:
Sankalp operates on a "Zero-Server" architecture. Your name, logs, reflections, and streaks are stored 100% locally on your device. We don't sell data because we don't collect it.
